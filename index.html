Welcome!

Hi, I am a Ph.D. scholar at International Max Planck Research School for Intelligent Systems (IMPRS-IS) and a Ph.D. candidate at the Human Computer Interaction and Cognitive Systems (HCI CS) department at the University of Stuttgart, Germany. My work aims to improve human-AI cooperation by investigating the intersection of Machine Theory of Mind and Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning. You can find more about me or my work on the about page, on our groups website, or on google scholar. For students interested in a project or thesis please check the open thesis page (only accessible from within the universities network).
